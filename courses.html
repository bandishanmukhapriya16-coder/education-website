Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

A high school diploma with minimum 75% aggregate, an admission test, and a personal interview. International students require equivalent qualifications.

Are scholarships available?

Yes. We offer merit-based scholarships covering up to 100% tuition, plus need-based aid and sports excellence awards.

Can I switch programs after enrollment?

Program transfers are possible during the first semester with academic counselor approval, subject to seat availability.

Do you offer hostel and accommodation?

Yes, our on-campus residence halls offer single and shared rooms with dining, study lounges, gym and 24/7 security.

What is the placement support like?

Our dedicated career cell partners with 120+ companies and provides resume workshops, mock interviews and internship pipelines.
